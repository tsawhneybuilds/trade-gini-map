Exercise 12: Where Export Growth Comes From

This rd2-only extension decomposes country export growth into new products, new partners, and new product-partner cells. The preferred product identity is harmonized HS6 family; HS4 and HS2 are robustness checks.

Mutually exclusive accounting rule

ΔXc,t,h = Σp,k(xc,p,k,t+h - xc,p,k,t)

Future cells are assigned in this order: product absent in the base year, then partner absent among existing products, then new product-partner cell where both product and partner existed, then already-active cell growth or contraction. This avoids double-counting. HS6 999999 is excluded before product-dependent aggregation.

  • Across country-year 5-year windows, median gross-positive expansion from net-new products is 28.2%.
  • Median gross-positive expansion from net-new partners for existing products is 0.1%.
  • Median gross-positive expansion from new cells among already-existing products and partners is 8.7%.
  • Validation: 60 rd2 countries, 161,654,903 product-partner source rows, and 0 HS6 999999 rows after filtering.
